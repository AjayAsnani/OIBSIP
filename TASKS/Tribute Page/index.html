SHAH RUKH KHAN
2 November 1965
ABOUT

Shah Rukh Khan, also known as the "Badshah of Bollywood" and "King Khan," is one of the most iconic and successful actors in the world. With a career spanning over three decades, he has starred in over 80 films, won numerous awards, and amassed a global fan following.

Khan is known for his versatility as an actor, having played a wide range of roles from romantic heroes to action stars to villains. He is also known for his charisma, wit, and charm.

Some of Shah Rukh Khan's most popular films include "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge," "Kuch Kuch Hota Hai," "Devdas," "Swades," "My Name is Khan," and "Chennai Express." He has also starred in several international films, including "Ashoka the Great" and "The Namesake."

In addition to his acting career, Shah Rukh Khan is also a successful businessman and philanthropist. He is the owner of the Indian Premier League cricket team Kolkata Knight Riders and the production company Red Chillies Entertainment. He is also the founder of the Meer Foundation, a non-profit organization that provides education and healthcare to underprivileged children.

Shah Rukh Khan is an inspiration to millions of people around the world. He is a living example of the power of hard work, dedication, and self-belief. He is also a role model for his humility, kindness, and compassion.
